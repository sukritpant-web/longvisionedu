Studying in the UK for Nepalese Students in 2025

Explore world-class education and international opportunities in the United Kingdom

Admission Requirements

  • Completed higher secondary education (10+2) with good grades
  • English language proficiency (IELTS, TOEFL, or PTE Academic)
  • Academic transcripts and certificates
  • Statement of Purpose
  • Letters of recommendation

Popular Courses

Popular Fields of Study:

  • Business and Management
  • Engineering
  • Computer Science and IT
  • Healthcare and Nursing
  • Environmental Science

Costs

  • Tuition fees: £10,000 to £20,000 per year for undergraduate programs, £11,000 to £25,000 for postgraduate programs
  • Living expenses: £10,000 to £15,000 per year
  • Visa application fee: £490

Scholarships

Several scholarships are available for Nepalese students:

  • GREAT Scholarships
  • Chevening Scholarships
  • Commonwealth Scholarships
  • University-specific scholarships

Visa Process

Nepalese students need to apply for a UK Student Visa. The process includes:

  • Receive a Confirmation of Acceptance for Studies (CAS) from your chosen university
  • Prepare required documents (passport, CAS, financial proof, English test scores)
  • Apply online and pay the visa fee (£490)
  • Attend a biometric appointment and possibly a visa interview

Work Opportunities

  • You can work up to 20 hours per week during term time and full-time during holidays.
  • The Graduate Route allows graduates to work in the UK for up to 2 years after completing their degree.

Tips for Nepalese Students

  • Start planning and applying at least a year in advance.
  • Research thoroughly about programs and institutions.
  • Improve English language skills.
  • Seek guidance from authorized education consultants.
  • Connect with current Nepalese students in the UK for insights.
