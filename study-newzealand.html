Study in New Zealand for Nepalese Students

Discover world-class education combined with stunning natural beauty and a welcoming multicultural environment.

Why Choose New Zealand?

  • High-quality education system with globally recognized qualifications
  • Safe and peaceful environment
  • Diverse, multicultural society
  • Stunning landscapes and outdoor adventures
  • Post-study work opportunities
  • English-speaking country

Popular Fields of Study

  • Business and Management
  • Engineering and Technology
  • Computer Science and IT
  • Environmental Studies
  • Health Sciences

Admission Requirements

  • Completed higher secondary education (10+2) with good grades
  • English language proficiency (IELTS, TOEFL, or PTE Academic)
  • Statement of Purpose
  • Letters of recommendation
  • Valid passport

Visa Process

The process to apply for a student visa includes:

  • Receive an offer letter from a New Zealand institution.
  • Apply for a student visa online and provide required documents:
    • - Offer of place
    - Proof of sufficient funds
    - Medical and police certificates
    - English language test results

Scholarships for Nepalese Students

Nepalese students can apply for these scholarships:

  • New Zealand International Doctoral Research Scholarships (NZIDRS)
  • New Zealand Commonwealth Scholarship and Fellowship Plan (CSFP)
  • University-specific scholarships

Work Opportunities

  • You can work part-time up to 20 hours per week during studies.
  • You can work full-time during scheduled breaks.
  • A post-study work visa allows you to work in New Zealand for 1-3 years after graduation.

Nepalese Community Support

Nepalese student associations in major cities organize cultural events and provide support networks for new students.

Tips for Nepalese Students

  • Start planning and applying at least a year in advance.
  • Research thoroughly about programs and institutions.
  • Improve your English language skills.
  • Connect with current Nepalese students in New Zealand for insights.
  • Prepare for the different climate and culture.
Studying in New Zealand offers Nepalese students an exceptional educational experience, combining world-class academics with stunning natural beauty and a welcoming multicultural environment. With proper planning and preparation, you can embark on a transformative journey in New Zealand.
